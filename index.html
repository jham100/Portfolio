My Story


I am originally from the New York City metropolitan area, where I graduated from SUNY Purchase in 2016 with a BA in Economics.

I relocated to Oakland, CA in early 2017 to fill an administrative position at Monterey Tilth: a large-scale cannabis cultivation company based in Salinas, CA.

I became immersed in software development at Monterey Tilth, where my role included data management and analysis from the start and grew into a technical role over time. At Monterey Tilth, I lead the development of an internal application for mission-critical business operations.

I attended the UC Berkeley Extention Full-Stack Web Development program from February to August of 2018 to widen my understanding of web development.

In September of 2019, I began a contract role as a Software Developer at a top University. In this role, I work with a team of Software Developers, Project Managers, QA Specialists and GIS specialists to build and support applications that facilitate the management and use of the University's physical assets.
